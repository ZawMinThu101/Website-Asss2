OUR STORY

CAFE & BAKERY DESSERTS

Zaw Min Thu and his partner Glenn founded Mara’s Café & Bakery in 1984 at the urging of family and friends. Right from the start, Zaw Min Thu began offering customers the highest quality, delectable desserts made fresh in our own bakery, from traditional family recipes. Glenn, a coffee perfectionist, distinguished a unique line of roasted and flavored coffee products using a small industrial roaster.

After nearly twenty years of establishing a reputation for serving up the best coffee and desserts in town, Zaw Min Thu knew it was time to expand not only the business, but the menu as well.
